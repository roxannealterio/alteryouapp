Who built this

Built by women,
for women

ALTER was built by Roxy and Cheyenne, two trainers who met on the gym floor at World Gym, personally training women face to face. That is where it all started, and it is where the app comes from: real women, in front of us, at every stage there is. Wanting to lose 30 kilos. Wanting to build muscle. Postpartum. Sixty and never having touched a barbell. Almost all of them arrived believing their body was a problem to be solved.

So many came already hurt, lifting with form that was quietly injuring them, because nobody had ever shown them how. That is why ALTER has a full form library, on every single exercise.

We both came through our own eating disorders. We know what it costs to spend years at war with your own body, and we are not building an app that starts that fight in anybody else. That is the whole reason ALTER behaves the way it does.

Chey has just had a baby, and coaching her own body through pregnancy and postpartum has only deepened what this is about. Strength through every season a woman goes through, not in spite of it.

Before you sign up

ALTER is not
for everyone

ALTER is right for you if
  • You want to lose fat and keep it off
  • You want to build strength, not simply become smaller
  • You have trained consistently and seen little change
  • You can give it two sessions a week, not five
ALTER is not right for you if
  • You want rapid weight loss. Fat loss is fully supported here, but it is measured in months.
  • You want a meal plan that bans entire food groups.
  • You want a program you can finish. This one does not end.
  • You are struggling with food or your body right now. That deserves proper support, not an app. Please read this first.
