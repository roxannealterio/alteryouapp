How it runs

Four steps, every round.

  1. 01

    Register in the app

    Tap join before the start date and lock your spot. Everyone begins together on day one.

  2. 02

    Get your plan

    Training and macros built around your answers. You are never left guessing what to do.

  3. 03

    Train with the cohort

    Log every session, chase your numbers, and lean on a whole community pushing the same weeks.

  4. 04

    Finish and compare

    Take your after photo and look at the data. The biggest transformation, by work done, takes the prize.

The prize

Judged on the work,
never on a body.

Every round has a winner, and it is never "who looks best". The prize goes to the biggest transformation measured the only way that is fair: the strength you built and the sessions you showed up for. Progress you earned, not a number on a scale.
