How do I delete my account?

Settings, then Delete my account. It removes everything, including your progress photos. It is immediate and permanent.

Are my progress photos private?

Yes. They are stored so that only your account can read them. No other user can see them, and they are deleted with your account.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open your iPhone Settings, tap your name, then Subscriptions, then ALTER.

I paid but ALTER says I am not premium

Open the paywall and tap Restore purchases. If that does not fix it, email us and we will sort it out.

Where do my calorie targets come from?

Your height, weight, age, activity level and goal, using the Mifflin St Jeor equation. You can change any of it in Settings and the targets update.

Is ALTER medical advice?

No. It gives general fitness and wellbeing guidance. Cycle predictions are estimates and must not be used for contraception. Talk to a healthcare professional before starting a new programme.
